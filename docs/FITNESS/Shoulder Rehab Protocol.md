## 0. Medical Diagnosis & Injury Context
**Primary Diagnosis:** **Grade 1 Supraspinatus Tendinopathy (Rotator Cuff)**
* **Mechanism:** "Double Crush" Injury.
    * *Chronic:* Repetitive overhead loading (Furniture Delivery).
    * *Acute:* Direct impact trauma (Soccer fall/shoulder barge).
* **Key Symptoms:**
    * Pain in "Zone B" (Deltoid insertion/side of arm).
    * Painful Arc at full overhead extension (near ear).
    * **Load Intolerance:** Pain on eccentric lowering of Pronated Pull-ups (Internal Rotation).
* **Contraindications (DO NOT DO):**
    * Wide-grip Pull-ups (Compresses the supraspinatus).
    * Upright Rows (Impairs subacromial space).
    * Sleeping on the injured side.

## 5. Phase 1 Workouts (Weeks 1â€“4)

### MON: Strength A (Legs & Back Support)
*Goal: Fix "Aching Back" by strengthening glutes/hamstrings and mobilizing hips.*

1.  **Warm-up:** *Perform Daily Routine (Section 1).*
2.  **Ankle Rehab:** Single-leg balance on pillow (Eyes open -> Eyes closed). **3 x 30s per leg.**
3.  **Trap Bar (or DB) Deadlift:** **3 x 8 reps.**
    * *Cue: Hinge hips back. Do NOT squat the weight. Feel hamstrings.*
4.  **Split Squats (Bodyweight/Light DB):** **3 x 10 per leg.**
    * *Why: Opens tight hip flexors dynamically.*
5.  **Bird-Dog:** **3 x 10 per side.**
    * *Cue: Brace core. Do not let lower back arch. Essential for back pain.*
6.  **Cool Down:** Pigeon Stretch (2 mins/side).

### WED: Strength B (Shoulder Repair - Grade 1 Focus)
*Goal: Re-align Supraspinatus collagen. **NO PULL-UPS/CHIN-UPS YET.***

1.  **Warm-up:** *Perform Daily Routine (Section 1).*
2.  **Floor Press (Dumbbells):** **3 x 12 reps.**
    * *Why: Floor stops elbows from going too deep and straining the capsule.*
3.  **Chest-Supported Row (Incline Bench):** **3 x 12 reps.**
    * *Why: Supports lower back. Focus on squeezing shoulder blades DOWN and back.*
4.  **Side-Lying Wiper (Eccentric Focus):** **3 x 10 reps.**
    * *The Healer:* Use light weight (1-2kg). Lift up with help, lower down SLOWLY (4 seconds) on your own.
5.  **Band Pull-Aparts:** **3 x 20 reps.**
    * *Cue: Palms up. Don't shrug shoulders.*

### FRI: Strength C (Full Body + Neck)
*Goal: Functional carrying capacity and Blueprint neck standards.*

1.  **Warm-up:** *Perform Daily Routine (Section 1).*
2.  **Goblet Squats (Heels Elevated if needed):** **3 x 12 reps.**
    * *Why: Front load forces upright posture, relieving back stress.*
3.  **Landmine Press (or Kneeling Single-Arm DB Press):** **3 x 10 per side.**
    * *Why: Pressing at an angle (not straight up) is safer for impingement.*
4.  **Suitcase Carries:** **3 sets x 30 meters.**
    * *Cue: Heavy DB in one hand. Walk perfectly straight. Anti-lateral flexion.*
5.  **Neck Harness (Flexion/Extension):** **2 x 15 reps (Light).**
    * *Bryan Johnson Standard:* Start very light. Control the tempo.
