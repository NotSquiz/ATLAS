# ATLAS Baseline Assessment Protocol v1.0
## Initialization Session - Day 0

**Subject:** Alexander, 38M  
**Date:** _____________  
**Purpose:** Establish quantitative baselines across all fitness domains before beginning Phase 1 training

---

## Overview

This protocol establishes measurable baselines for tracking progress throughout your 12-week Phase 1 and beyond. Re-test at:
- **Week 4** (partial - strength only)
- **Week 8** (partial - strength + cardio)  
- **Week 12** (full protocol - GATE 1 assessment)

**Session Structure:**
| Session | Focus | Duration | Equipment Needed |
|---------|-------|----------|------------------|
| Day 0-A | Body Comp + Movement Screen | 45 min | Scale, tape measure, phone (video) |
| Day 0-B | Strength Baselines | 60 min | Dumbbells, kettlebells, pull-up bar |
| Day 0-C | Cardio + Endurance | 45 min | Bike (or walk), stopwatch |

*Complete on separate days or with 4+ hours between sessions*

### A3. Flexibility & Mobility Tests

#### Weight-Bearing Lunge Test (WBLT) - Ankle Dorsiflexion
*Critical for ankle rehab tracking*

**Protocol:**
1. Face wall, foot pointing straight ahead
2. Lunge knee toward wall while keeping heel flat
3. Move foot back until knee just touches wall at max distance
4. Measure from big toe to wall (cm)

| Limb | Distance (cm) | Asymmetry |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| Right Ankle | _____ cm | |
| Left Ankle | _____ cm | |
| **Difference** | _____ cm | Target: <2cm |

*Reference: ≥10cm is normal. <2cm asymmetry required for GATE 1*

#### Sit-and-Reach Test - Hamstring/Lower Back
**Protocol:**
1. Sit with legs extended, feet against box/wall
2. Reach forward with both hands, keeping knees straight
3. Measure distance past toes (+ cm) or short of toes (- cm)

| Attempt | Distance |
|---------|----------|
| 1 | _____ cm |
| 2 | _____ cm |
| 3 | _____ cm |
| **Best** | _____ cm |

*Reference for 38M: >+5cm = Good, 0 to +5cm = Average, <0cm = Below Average*

#### Shoulder Mobility (Apley Scratch Test)
**Protocol:**
1. Right hand over right shoulder, reach down back
2. Left hand behind back, reach up
3. Measure gap between fingertips (or overlap)
4. Repeat opposite side

| Test | Distance |
|------|----------|
| Right over, Left under | _____ cm (gap/overlap) |
| Left over, Right under | _____ cm (gap/overlap) |
| **Asymmetry** | _____ cm |

*Note: Given shoulder injury, expect limitation on injured side*

#### Hip Flexor Length (Thomas Test - Modified)
**Protocol:**
1. Lie on edge of bed/bench, pull one knee to chest
2. Let other leg hang off edge
3. Observe: Does thigh stay flat? Does knee bend past 90°?

| Side | Thigh Position | Knee Angle | Notes |
|------|----------------|------------|-------|
| Right | Flat / Raised | >90° / <90° | |
| Left | Flat / Raised | >90° / <90° | |

*Raised thigh = tight hip flexors*

### C3. 3-Minute Step Test (Cardio Fitness)

**Protocol:**
1. Use 30cm (12") step
2. Step up/down to metronome at 96 bpm (24 steps/min)
3. Continue for 3 minutes
4. Stop, remain standing, measure HR after 1 minute of rest

|--------|-------|
| 1-min Recovery HR | _____ bpm |

*Reference (38M): <100 bpm = Excellent, 100-110 = Good, 110-120 = Average, >120 = Below Average*
